How It All Began

Year 2020, I turned 30.
30 is an age when most important decisions of life gets made including things such as a career and a marriage.
Things such as value, view, relationships and health status ultimately become the base of decisions.

For this reason having good goals, habits, hobbies are so important. And these are things that reflect one's value, view and so on.
Therefore, at the very beginning of year 2020, I have decided to set up some goals, hobbies and habits.
